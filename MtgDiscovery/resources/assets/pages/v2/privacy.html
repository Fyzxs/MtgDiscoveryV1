Privacy Policy

There isn't privacy on this site.

Assume everything done with the site is public.

You should assume that, because that's kinda the design of the site. The only info-security is obscurity.


Except your password. That's not stored. It's hashed and the hash is stored.

You should assume the hash is publicly available (though I do my best to not have that happen).

So don't re-use a password for this site.
